Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have to boost the server!

No, you are not required, unless you want to access perks when you server boost. But otherwise if you don't have nitro, you will never be required to boost the server.

Do we need nitro to join the server?

Absolutely not! Anyone can join! Nitro and members without nitro can join and it doesn't affect the server at all!
